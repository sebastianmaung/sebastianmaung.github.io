Manga and Anime

Jan 7, 2023

Last year, I didn't watch anime too much until a friend introduced me. I have only watched a few, like Pokemon and maybe a few others. But I eventually watched Attack on Titan, and I was hooked. I watched the entire thing in 1 month until season 4 part 1. When part 2 was released, I got bored. What I like is a sort of modern, realistic style. While titans won't be killing us and we won't be using jetpacks with swords and hooks anytime soon, it gives the feel of an organized society.

Like Naoki Urasawa's Monster, it's modern and could (maybe) happen in real life. Currently, I don't watch too much anime anymore. I instead read manga because it fits into my schedule easily. I read slice of life manga mostly, like GTO, Nichijou, Your Lie in April, etc. I think I will branch out and start reading new things outside of this, but for now I will stick to this for probably the rest of school. It's good to have something to relate to and relax with when stressed.
